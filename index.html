Since 2021

Four Years of Connecting Families
to Their Roots

The Online Master was founded in 2021 by six mompreneurs who shared one conviction — that children growing up abroad should never lose their language, their culture, or their roots. What began as a small Tamil tutoring initiative has grown into a thriving virtual school with students in over 21 countries across the USA, UK, Australia, UAE, Singapore, Canada, and beyond.

We offer five thoughtfully designed courses — Tamil, Hindi, Malayalam, Maths, and Crochet — taught by qualified, passionate teachers in flexible 1:1 private sessions or small group batches. Every class is scheduled around your family's time zone, and every child gets the attention they deserve.

Our Story

Born Out of Passion & Purpose

The Online Master was born from the shared dream of six visionary mompreneurs — Dr. Ponni, Cinthamani, Selvi, Asha, Abhi, and Varshni. As mothers raising children abroad, they faced a common challenge: the lack of quality Tamil language education for diaspora kids.

What started as a Tamil language platform quickly expanded to Hindi, Malayalam, Maths and Crochet — driven by overwhelming demand from parents worldwide. Today, The Online Master is a community-driven school, rooted in culture and powered by passionate educators.
